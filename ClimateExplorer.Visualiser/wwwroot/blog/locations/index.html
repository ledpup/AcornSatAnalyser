    Locations

    Climate Explorer has 139 locations from Australia and New Zealand. These locations are sourced from the Bureau of Meteorology and the National Institute of Water and Atmospheric. There is currently one location from the United Kingdom, HadCET (Hadley Centre Central England Temperature), included as a reference site. HadCET is the oldest, continuously operating, record of temperature in the world.
